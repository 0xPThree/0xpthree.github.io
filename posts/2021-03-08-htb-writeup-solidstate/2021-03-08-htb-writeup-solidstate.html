  1. Quick look at JAMES 2.3.2 we find a authenticated RCE (35513.py) vuln

[p3:/git/htb/solidstate]$ searchsploit JAMES — snip — Apache James Server 2.3.2 - Remote Command Execution | linux/remote/35513.py

The script logs in with default credentials (root:root) and creates the user ‘../../../../../../../../etc/bash_completion.d’. Once created, an email containing a malicious payload will be sent which will be triggered once someone logs into the machine.
